## ğŸŒŸ Features / Highlights

### Business Problem

Retail businesses often struggle to quickly understand revenue trends, product performance, customer behavior, and operational efficiency across multiple stores. Key questions like:

* Which products generate the most revenue or profit?
* How do returns affect overall sales performance?
* Which stores outperform others, and why?
* What are customer purchase patterns by segment or loyalty?

â€¦are hard to answer with raw data.

### Goal of the Dashboard

To provide an interactive tool that:

* Tracks sales, profit, and returns across stores and product categories.
* Helps identify trends in customer behavior, transaction volume, and product performance.
* Supports data-driven decisions for inventory planning, marketing, and store operations.

### Walkthrough of Key Visuals

* **Key KPIs (Top Panel)**

  * Total Revenue, Total Profit, Total Returns, Return Rate
  * Quantity Sold, Number of Transactions, Unique Products
  * Weekend vs. Weekday Transactions

* **Time Analysis**

  * Monthly and quarterly revenue trends with YoY% and MoM% changes
  * 3-month moving averages and 60-day rolling revenue

* **Product Insights**

  * Revenue and profit by product category and type (bar and line charts)
  * Top-selling products vs. products with highest returns

* **Customer Insights**

  * Segmentation by loyalty, purchase frequency, and demographics
  * Average transaction value and return behavior

* **Store Performance**

  * Revenue, profit, and return rates per store (map and bar charts)
  * Store comparison by operational metrics

* **Interactive Filters & Slicers**

  * Filter by store, product category, customer segment, and time period
  * Cross-filtering enables drill-down and dynamic analysis

### Business Impact & Insights

* **Revenue Optimization:** Identify top-performing products, stores, and customer segments to maximize sales and profitability.
* **Operational Efficiency:** Pinpoint stores or product lines with high return rates or low performance.
* **Customer Strategy:** Enhance loyalty programs by understanding customer purchase behavior and trends.
* **Decision Support:** Management can make informed choices on promotions, inventory, staffing, and regional expansion.
